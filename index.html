Pngs (portable-network-images) are anonymous, circulated, public, generic.

Pngs are contradictory, they appear to be a public resource. But the watermarks implies that they are mastered, preventing you from getting a “perfect” image.

Pngs are a fiction, even the transparent grid in the background of the image is just a symbol and imitation of transparency.

Pngs are advertised as free, personal, capable, beautiful, all of these are idealized description.

At the same time they are not perfect, they are a mass-produced replica, they are constrained by pixels, reflecting the grids of different sizes.
